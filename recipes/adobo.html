Adobo

Description

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish that involves meat, seafood, or vegetables marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black peppercorns, which is browned in oil, and simmered in the marinade. It has a characteristically salty and sour flavor and is often served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 pound chicken legs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Add the chicken and turn to coat. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, heat the oil. Add the chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Flip the chicken and cook for 5 minutes more.
  4. Add the marinade, water, and onion. Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and simmer for 30 minutes, covered, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a serving platter, arrange the chicken. Serve hot.
